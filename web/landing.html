Neighborhood Safety Insights

Understand the Area Before You Move In

We analyze real UK crime data to give each neighborhood a safety score from 1 to 10. Instantly visualize high- and low-risk areas on the map to make informed decisions when buying or renting a property.

Market Trends & Property Prices

Track Real-Time Price Fluctuations

Using verified housing and rental data from gov.uk, we provide up-to-date market trends, average property prices, and rental rates for every borough—so you never overpay or miss out on a good deal.

Accessible Design

Designed with Visual Inclusivity in Mind

Our map is thoughtfully built for users with different types of colour vision. We use contrast-friendly palettes and pattern-based indicators so everyone can easily interpret safety scores and property insights—no one left out.
